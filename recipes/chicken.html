Spicy Chicken

Description

Learn how to make Spicy and tasty chicken sabji

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast - 250gms
  • Medium sized onion - 1
  • Vegetable oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil the chicken breast in a container until it is cooked.
  2. After its done let it cool down.
  3. Take out the cooked chicken breast in separate container/plate.
  4. With you hand make small pieces of the breast, more like squish the chicken breast.
  5. Fine chop 1 medium sized onion.
  6. Place a cooking utensil on a gas let it heat up.
  7. Add 10 ml of vegetable oil.
  8. Add the finely chopped onion to the utensil and cook until golden brown.
  9. Add spices of your choice.
  10. Add the small pieces of chicken breast to the container and cook it well.
