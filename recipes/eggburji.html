Egg Burji

Description

Ingredients and steps to make tasty, healthy and low calorie egg burji.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium sized onions
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 or 2 spicy chilli
  • 3 eggs
  • butter
  • Spices

Steps

Follow the steps as they are.

  1. Fine chop 2 medium sized onions
  2. Fine chop a tamato
  3. Fine chop a chilli
  4. Put the pan on the gas and let it heat up.
  5. Add 10gms of butter to the pan.
  6. Add chopped onions and let them cook properly on low medium heat. Cook them till they turn dark golden.
  7. Add chopped tomato and chilli, and mix them well
  8. Add your favoured spices and salt for taste and mix.
  9. Add the eggs to the pan.
  10. Keep stiring the burji until it loses its water content.
